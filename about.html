OUR STORY

10 Years of Precision
Estimating Excellence

Founded in 2016, AccuBuild Estimating was built on a single premise: construction professionals deserve fast, accurate, and affordable estimating services that give them a real competitive edge.

We've grown from a small team of three estimators into a nationwide network of 50+ certified construction cost professionals covering every trade and every project type — from single-family homes to large industrial complexes.

Our estimators follow the strict guidelines of the American Association of Cost Engineers (AACE) and use proprietary zip-code-based cost databases to ensure every estimate reflects current local market conditions.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your standard turnaround time? +

Our standard turnaround is 24–48 hours from the time we receive your plans and project details. For urgent bids, we offer priority 12-hour service on Professional and Enterprise plans. Large complex projects may require up to 72 hours.

What file formats do you accept? +

We accept PDF and most image formats. You can submit plans via email, WeTransfer, Dropbox, or our upload portal. We can also access plans from Procore, PlanGrid, or any web-based document management system.

What software do you use for estimating? +

We use PlanSwift and Bluebeam for accurate digital takeoffs and detailed quantity measurements, along with Excel for precise calculations and cost analysis, and can also work with client-specific software as required.

How accurate are your estimates? +

Our estimates are typically within 5–10% of actual bid prices. We use location-specific databases to ensure pricing reflects your local market.

Do you offer revisions? +

Yes—all plans include revisions. Starter plans include at least 5 revisions, Professional plans include up to 10 revisions, and addenda with minor changes are handled at no cost, while larger scope changes are priced separately at a minimal, transparent fee.

Can I get a dedicated estimator? +

Absolutely. Our Enterprise plan assigns a dedicated construction estimator who works exclusively on your projects. They'll learn your bidding preferences, company standards, and preferred suppliers to deliver consistent results every time.
