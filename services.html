Our Services

WHAT WE OFFER

Complete Estimating Solutions
for Every Phase

AccuBuild Estimating provides a full suite of construction cost estimating and takeoff services. Our certified estimators use industry-leading tools including PlanSwift, Bluebeam and RS Means to deliver precision estimates in 24–48 hours.

DIVISION 01

Cost Estimating

Building Cost Estimating

Comprehensive building cost estimates covering all phases, from schematic design through construction documents.

  • Location-specific material pricing
  • Labor cost databases
  • RS Means integration
  • CSI division breakdown
  • Change order management

Bid Preparation and submission support

Preparation and submission of a comprehensive bid report outlining proposal details, compliance, pricing, and supporting documentation.

  • Bid forms completion
  • Scope Review
  • Inclusions/Exclusions
  • Proposal packaging
  • Subcontractor comparison sheets

Budget Estimating

Early-stage budget estimates using conceptual or schematic drawings to establish financial feasibility.

  • Conceptual estimates
  • Order of magnitude pricing
  • Value engineering support
  • Feasibility analysis
  • Change order management
DIVISION 02

Construction Estimating

Freelance Estimating

On-demand estimating support for contractors who need extra capacity during peak bidding seasons.

  • Per-project pricing
  • All trades covered
  • Flexible engagement

Dedicated Estimator

A dedicated construction estimator assigned exclusively to your projects for consistent quality and relationship.

  • Single point of contact
  • Deep project knowledge
  • Monthly retainer available

Blueprint Estimating

Complete plan review and takeoff from architectural, structural, MEP, and civil drawings.

  • Full plan review
  • All trade coordination
  • Detailed line items
  • Monthly retainers for consistent estimating support, from fixed to unlimited.
DIVISION 03

Specialized Estimating

Commercial Estimating

Office towers, retail centers, hotels, warehouses — commercial project estimates for any scale.

  • Accurate quantity takeoffs
  • Detailed cost estimates (CSI format)
  • Subcontractor scope review
  • Material & labor cost analysis
  • Change order evaluation
  • Fast turnaround & clear reporting

Residential Estimating

Single-family homes to large multi-family complexes. Detailed estimates for every residential project.

  • Complete material & quantity takeoffs
  • Project-specific cost estimates
  • Change order evaluation
  • Budget updates & cost tracking
  • Accurate, on-time delivery

Industrial Estimating

Refineries, manufacturing plants, warehouses — heavy industrial estimates by certified engineers.

  • Project scope review & quantity takeoffs
  • Detailed cost breakdowns
  • Change order evaluation
  • Installation & fabrication pricing
  • Engineering accuracy you can rely on

Construction Takeoff

Comprehensive quantity takeoffs for materials, labor, and equipment for any trade.

  • Digital plan takeoffs
  • All CSI divisions
  • Quantity verification
