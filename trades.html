Trade Estimating Services

Concrete Estimating

Footings, slabs, walls, columns, and structural concrete takeoffs with formwork and rebar.

Electrical Estimating

Complete electrical takeoffs including conduit, wire, panels, lighting, and gear for any project type.

Masonry Estimating

CMU block, brick, stone, and mortar takeoffs with labor productivity factors by region.

HVAC Estimating

Ductwork, equipment, piping, and controls estimates using FastDUCT for residential and commercial systems.

Plumbing Estimating

Domestic water, DWV, sanitary, storm, and medical gas systems with fixture counts and rough-in.

Mechanical Estimating

Process piping, HVAC, industrial mechanical systems — detailed estimates using FastPIPE.

Roofing Estimating

TPO, EPDM, metal, shingle, and flat roof systems with insulation and flashing takeoffs.

Sitework Estimating

Earthwork, grading, utilities, paving, and landscaping takeoffs for civil site packages.

Drywall Takeoff

Metal framing, drywall, insulation, and ceiling system takeoffs for interior build-outs.

Lumber Takeoff

Wood framing, engineered lumber, trusses, and rough carpentry material lists and costs.

Insulation Estimating

Batt, blown-in, spray foam, and rigid insulation takeoffs for walls, roofs, and mechanical systems.

Flooring Estimating

Carpet, tile, hardwood, LVT, polished concrete, and specialty flooring systems estimated precisely.

Other Trades Estimating
